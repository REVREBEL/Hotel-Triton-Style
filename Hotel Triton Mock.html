Our Philosophy

Classic Bones.
Creative Soul.

Hotel Triton isn't just a place to sleep—it's a living gallery of San Francisco's eclectic spirit. We've stripped back the ordinary to reveal a space that honors the city's artistic heritage while offering the warm, residential comfort of a friend's well-curated apartment.

From the globally-inspired lobby to the thoughtful details in every room, we invite you to experience a stay that is both culturally rich and effortlessly welcoming.
